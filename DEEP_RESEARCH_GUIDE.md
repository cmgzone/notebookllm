## âœ¨ Key Features

### 1. Context Engineering Agent
When enabled, the agent analyzes your research topic to generate:
- **Core Concepts**: Fundamental ideas you need to grasp.
- **Prerequisites**: What you should know before diving in.
- **Learning Path**: A step-by-step roadmap to mastery.
- **Common Pitfalls**: Mistakes to avoid.

- **Mindmap**: A visual representation of the topic's structure (Mermaid.js).

### 2. Mindmap Generation
The agent automatically creates a visual mindmap of the research topic, helping you understand the relationships between different concepts at a glance.

### 3. Save to Notebook
You can now save the entire research report (including the mindmap and context analysis) directly to one of your notebooks. This turns your research into a permanent knowledge asset.
