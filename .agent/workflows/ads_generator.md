# Ads Generator Workflow

1.  **Navigate to Ads Generator**:
    - Open the app.
    - Open the side drawer (hamburger menu).
    - Scroll down to the "Tools" section.
    - Tap on "Ads Generator".

2.  **Generate Text-Only Ad**:
    - Enter your product description or ad requirements in the text field.
    - Tap "Generate Ad".
    - Wait for the AI to generate the headline, body, and CTA.

3.  **Generate Ad with Photo (Multimodal)**:
    - Tap the "Add Product Photo" area (or placeholder icon).
    - Select an image from your device.
    - Enter any specific requirements in the text field (optional, but recommended).
    - Tap "Generate Ad".
    - The AI will analyze the image and generate an ad relevant to the visual content.

4.  **View and Copy**:
    - The generated ad will appear below the button.
    - Use the "Copy" icon in the top right of the result card to copy the text to your clipboard.
